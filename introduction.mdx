# Welcome to MavunoX

MavunoX is a comprehensive machine learning system designed to predict harvest seasons and provide AI-powered farming recommendations to rural farmers across Africa. By analyzing environmental factors such as soil pH, water availability, temperature, and humidity, MavunoX helps farmers optimize crop yields through data-driven insights.

## How It Works

MavunoX combines three specialized machine learning models with GPT-based recommendations:

1. **Soil pH Predictor** - Converts RGB soil color values into pH estimates using XGBoost regression trained on soil color-to-pH mappings
2. **Water Availability Model** - Estimates water content from grayscale soil images using linear interpolation
3. **Main Harvest Predictor** - XGBoost classifier that predicts harvest seasons based on pH, water availability, crop type, and country (Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Sudan)
4. **Recommendation Engine** - OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo generates context-aware farming advice for temperature, humidity, pH, and water management

## Model Performance

The main harvest prediction model achieves:

- **Overall Accuracy**: 89%
- **Macro Average Precision**: 92%
- **Macro Average Recall**: 92%
- **Macro Average F1-Score**: 92%

<Note>
The model was trained on data from the [Klusterthon Hackathon](https://www.kluster.africa/problem-statements/precision-farming-for-best-product-results-with-data), focusing on environmental factors across four African countries.
</Note>

## Use Cases

- **Harvest Planning**: Predict optimal harvest seasons based on current environmental conditions
- **Soil Analysis**: Analyze soil pH and water content using smartphone camera images
- **Crop Optimization**: Get recommendations for improving temperature, humidity, pH, and water levels
- **Regional Adaptation**: Country-specific predictions for Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, and Sudan

## Technology Stack

- **Framework**: FastAPI with CORS support
- **ML Models**: XGBoost (classifier and regressor)
- **AI Engine**: OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo
- **Data Processing**: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn
- **Deployment**: Docker, Uvicorn
- **Python Version**: 3.9+
